Githeri Recipe

A delicacy best served with green sukumawiki,avocado and a cup of chai!

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of maize (corn) kernels
  • 1 cup of beans
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 2-3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 ripe tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon of cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon of ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Water for cooking

Instructions:

  1. If you are using dried beans, soak them in water overnight or for at least 6 hours. Drain and rinse before using.
  2. In a large pot, combine the maize and beans, then cover them with enough water to soak. Let them soak for about 2 hours.
  3. After soaking, drain and rinse the maize and beans once again.
  4. In a large pot, add the maize and beans and cover them with water. The water level should be about an inch above the maize and beans. Bring the mixture to a boil.
  5. Reduce the heat to low, cover the pot, and let it simmer for about 45 minutes to 1 hour or until the maize and beans are tender. Add more water if needed to keep the mixture from drying out.
